About us

Concept B is a family business that takes care of bees and produces all natural products that come from this amazing insect. 🐝
We also welcome families, students and anyone who is interested in learning more about nature and this wonderful insect in our bee farms in Dlebta, Kfardebian, Bikfaya, and Tannourine.

During these difficult times, we left Greece and came to Lebanon to start here, hoping and believing that this country would rise up again.
Concept B is your choice of 100% natural products just as nature and bees produce them.
